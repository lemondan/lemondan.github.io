Research Interest

My general research interest is to develop structured machine learning techniques for computer vision tasks. I mainly investigate how to exploit the human commonsense and incorporate them to develop the advanced artificial intelligence system. Recently, I focus on

  • Video Analysis, including detection, captioning, summarization and prediction.
  • Medical and Biological image analysis
  • Deep Learning, including Graph LSTM, structure learning
  • Human-centric Analysis, including 2D/3D human pose estimation, human parsing, face analysis
  • Unsupervised Learning
